Our Story

Born in Tripoli's ancient souks, preserved for the world.

Khan Al Saboun is a legendary Lebanese brand with over 600 years of history. Revitalized by Dr. Bader Hassoun, it preserves traditional soapmaking methods while transforming them into a global symbol of luxury, purity, and botanical care.
